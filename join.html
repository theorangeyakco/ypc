Join YPC

YPC is currently open for new applicants. We are a selective organization, and we’re looking for motivated and driven young people, aged 14-19, to join our team and help move our cause forward. We welcome global applicants, and there is no fee for application or membership at YPC.

Applicants can either choose to apply to one of our Committees or our Core Team. Details can be found below:


Our Process

Join a Committee
Role description:

  • Work closely with a team of 5-6 students on research projects within one of YPC’s five committees:
    • Economics and Trade
    • Gender and Inclusion
    • Environment and Sustainability
    • Health and Wellbeing
    • International Relations
  • Research projects follow YPC’s research process, which can be found here. Within this team, members of each committee can:
    • Discuss and deliberate pressing current issues that they are passionate about
    • Collect primary data through surveys and interviews, as well as find secondary data through journals and online sources
    • Collaborate with industry leaders and experts
    • Write research papers, articles and reports
    • Publish their research on external platforms or in YPC’s journal, website and newsletter.
    • Present their research, findings and recommendations to relevant policymaking institutions

Join Our Core Team
Role description:

  • Our Core Team is the heart of the organization, with three different sub-teams:
    • Administration and Community Engagement: Works on managing and organizing our community, as well as developing and implementing strategies to drive member engagement.
    • Social Media and Design: Manages YPC’s social media accounts, designs and develops content such as our journal, newsletter and reports
    • Outreach: Fosters engagement with YPC's external stakeholders, such as NGOs, government agencies and companies, to form positive, symbiotic partnerships and explore collaboration opportunities.

Requirements

  • Applicants must:
    • Be aged 14-19 years old
    • Have good writing and communication skills
    • Be able to work in groups online
    • Be able to commit 6-8 hours per week while involved in a research project. We recognize that most applicants are students, and we are flexible in terms of engagement.
  • Applicants are not required to have any prior research experience.

Requirements

  • Applicants must:
    • Be aged 14-19 years old
    • Have excellent communication and organizational skills
    • Be able to demonstrate their proficiency in the role they have applied for
  • Previous experience in a similar role is preferred
  • Time commitment varies depending on the role chosen. We would be happy to clarify any questions applicants may have on a case-by-case basis.
