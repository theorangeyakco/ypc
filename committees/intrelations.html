International Relations

Fostering productive and healthy relationships between countries is vital as it helps the global community address issues at scale through positive collaboration. However, the world is currently at a tipping point; international relationships are more strained than they have been in decades and are poised to deteriorate further. Isolationism and authoritarianism are on the rise, severely limiting our ability to solve problems through global collaboration.

Our International Relations committee will explore the complexities and nuances of global politics and develop new perspectives on how global collaboration can be used to address the pervasive issues currently facing our global community.
