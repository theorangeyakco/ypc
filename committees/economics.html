Economics and Trade

Economics and trade are intricately linked to the development of our communities as well as the improvement of global living standards. The complexity of the global economy and the forces that govern it lead to the creation of a variety of issues which need policy intervention and regulation.

Our Economics and Trade committee will explore a wide range of issues regarding global and regional economies from both a micro and macro lens. We develop perspectives and possible policies to facilitate a more efficient, productive economy to bring about growth and development.
