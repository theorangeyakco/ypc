Health and Wellbeing

Health and wellbeing are essential for human happiness and progress. In our modern world, issues such as mental health, dietary illnesses and disease outbreaks are plaguing society and limiting human potential. A broad range of policy change and action is required to help our global community overcome the challenges and nurture a healthy, happy population.

Our Health and Wellbeing Committee will explore a wide range of issues pertaining to the physical and mental health of people across all segments of our communities.
