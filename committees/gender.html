Gender and Inclusion

As our world becomes increasingly diverse, policy needs to be created which fosters inclusiveness and equity throughout society. Our Gender and Inclusion committee explores issues related to race, religion, gender, sexual orientation, caste and age.

As our generation continues to explore the components which make up our identity, we bridge the gaps between people from different backgrounds and create a truly united global community.
